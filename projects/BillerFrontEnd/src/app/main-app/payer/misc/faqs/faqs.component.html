1. What is E-biller?

  • E-biller is an account management system for business and corporate clients which makes the process of paying bulk electricity bills easier.

    • 2. Is the E-biller portal available for both my organization’s post pay and prepaid meter accounts and bill payment?

  • E-biller portal is only available for postpaid accounts

    • 3. How can I pay my bills using E-biller portal

  • Log into E-biller portal, select the Maintain Accounts and register your Kenya power bill accounts
  • On the payment menu, select “make payment option” and generate an E-slip
  • Log in as an authorizer and approve the e-slip
  • Print the E-slip and make a payment through available channels to Kenya Power account details provided in the E-slip.

    • 4. What options are available for bill payment?

  • RTGS
  • Online Banking
  • Mpesa
  • Cash and Cheque deposits at any of Stanbic Bank branch countrywide

    • 5. Is there a fee charged to my organization for using E-biller portal?

  • No, the service is free of charge

    • 6. How will my organization benefit for using E-biller portal?

  • Easier payment of bulk bills: there is no need to forwarding bill schedules to KPLC.
  • Easier management of bills.
  • No more laborious manual reconciliation of customer bill accounts
  • Real time updating of accounts.
  • Better accountability for payments: the system provides a maker and checker capability in order to validate E-slips before payments

    • 8. How will E-biller portal help my organization manage its power accounts billing cycle?

  • Clients are able to view their accounts bills at any time of the day and pay without delay
  • Clients no longer have to rely on manual bills from Kenya Power
